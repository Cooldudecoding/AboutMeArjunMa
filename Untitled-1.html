These activities have helped me focus in school and have better time management than most of my peers.

I am very collaborative and like working with many different types of people but I can also work independently as well. I am currently on the football team and from this I have learned that everybody needs to pull their weight and no one can take a day off if you are on a team. This has helped me become a better team player with a greater motivation to work hard and get things done. All in all, my experiences and extracurricular activities have shaped who I am today and will continue to shape me in the future.
